What the App Does (Authorized Functions) The app performs **only** the following five categories of operation, all on the user's explicit instruction, on the user's own accounts: 1. **Upload videos** to the user's Facebook Pages, Instagram Business / Creator account, and TikTok account. 2. **Publish posts** — text posts, photo posts, and video posts (including Reels). 3. **Edit videos** — trim, crop, merge clips, add text overlay. 4. **Edit video metadata** — title, caption / description, hashtags, thumbnail / cover, privacy setting. 5. **Reply to audience comments** on the user's own published content. --- ## 4. What the App Does NOT Do (Prohibited Functions) The app **does not** perform any of the following: - Reading friends lists, followers, contact lists, or private messages. - Performing mass automated actions (mass-following, mass-liking, mass-commenting). - Targeting users with advertising, retargeting, lookalike modelling, or attribution measurement. - Reselling, transferring, or sharing any platform data with third parties, data brokers, advertising networks, or AI training pipelines. - Performing surveillance, background checks, facial recognition, or any biometric processing. - Acting on accounts the connecting user does not own or have written authorization to manage. These restrictions are enforced both technically (the app's backend only accepts requests matching the five authorized functions) and contractually (the Terms of Service §3 prohibits them). --- ## 5.
