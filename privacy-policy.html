🔒 Privacy Policy

Qeloria Content Publisher — Last updated: June 2025

Summary: We collect the minimum data necessary to publish your educational content to TikTok. We do not sell, share, or distribute any personal information.

1. Information We Collect

Qeloria Content Publisher collects only the following information:

  • TikTok Authorization: OAuth2 access token and refresh token required to publish videos on your behalf.
  • Content Data: Video files, titles, descriptions, and tags that you explicitly provide for publishing.
  • Basic Profile: Your TikTok open ID and basic account info provided through the TikTok API.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate your TikTok account via OAuth2
  • To upload and publish video content you create to your TikTok account
  • To manage and track published content

3. What We Do NOT Do

  • We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information
  • We do not share your data with third parties
  • We do not access your private messages or contacts
  • We do not collect browsing history or device information
  • We do not use your data for advertising purposes

4. Data Storage & Security

All authentication tokens are stored securely with encryption on our private servers. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

5. Data Retention

We retain your OAuth tokens only as long as necessary to provide the service. You may revoke access at any time through your TikTok account settings, which immediately invalidates all stored tokens.

6. Third-Party Services

This application integrates with the TikTok Content Posting API. Your use of TikTok is governed by TikTok's own Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

7. Your Rights

  • Revoke access at any time via TikTok settings
  • Request deletion of all stored data
  • Contact us with any privacy concerns

8. Children's Privacy

While our content is educational and family-friendly, this tool is intended for use by adult content creators. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

9. Contact

For privacy inquiries, please contact: qeloria.publisher@gmail.com
